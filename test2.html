First, to play Truth or Dare, you need to have at least two players. You can have as many players as you want...

'Truth or Dare' is made by Arto-Pekka Hänninen and Juuso Heikkinen, both students of Haaga-Helia's School of applied sciences.

Making this game was a part of a course called "Mobiilituotekehitys".

We wish you and your friends funny gaming moments with our version of 'Truth or Dare'!
